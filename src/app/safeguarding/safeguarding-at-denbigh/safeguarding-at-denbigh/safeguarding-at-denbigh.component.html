Child Protection Policy/Guidence

At Denbigh Primary School we recognise our moral and statutory responsibility to safeguard and promote the welfare of all pupils. We endeavour to provide a safe and welcoming environment where children are respected and valued. We are alert to the signs of abuse and neglect and follow our procedures to ensure that children receive effective support, protection and justice.

Safeguarding our children is the responsibility of every member of staff at Denbigh. We have a dedicated safeguarding team of staff who work together to ensure the safety of all children at Denbigh.
