Breakfast Club

Breakfast Club runs daily from 08:00 until 08:35. It is staffed by at five members of school staff. Breakfast club is open to children from Reception to Year 6. Breakfast Club is free of charge for all pupils. A non-compulsory breakfast is offered to children who attend and includes toast, fruit and cereal. A variety of activities are on offer each day.
