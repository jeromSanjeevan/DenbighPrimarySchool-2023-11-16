PE kit

PE is an essential part of a child’s education; not only does it highlight the importance of physical health, it teaches teamwork, leadership skills and supports mental health. Wearing a PE kit to school is great for the subject, it raises the profile of PE and places it at the forefront of learning. It also shows PE is valued in your school and highlights how important PE is for a healthy active lifestyle.

Pupils should wear the following PE Kit to school on their designated PE days which will ensure that the pupils can take full advantage of their PE slot.

GIRL -

Red t-shirt (with school logo)

Navy Blue red 3⁄4 zip sports top (with school logo)

Navy Blue sports trouser (with school logo)

Navy Blue or Black leggings or jogging bottoms

Trainers

BOY -

Red t-shirt (with school logo)

Navy Blue red 3⁄4 zip sports top (with school logo)

Navy Blue sports trouser (with school logo)

Navy Blue or Black shorts or jogging bottoms

Trainers

Swimming Lessons
Swimming Lessons (part of the national curriculum) Swimsuit and towel Swimming Kit Bag with school logo - or other suitable bag for wet swimming kit
JEWELLERY, HAIR AND MAKE UP:

For safety reasons, children must not wear jewellery to school (except for watches). they have pierced ears, children are only allowed to wear a single set of small studs, which must be removed for P.E sessions. Children who wear any other jewellery will be asked to remove it.

Hair cuts should be suitable for school - we actively discourage fashion fads (e.g. shaved designs and dyed hair) and we will ask you to get it cut out at the hairdressers.

Make up and nail varnish are not considered appropriate for school.

Uniform is available for parents to purchase from the school office.
