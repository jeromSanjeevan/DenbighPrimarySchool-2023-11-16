School Day

The School Day - Pupils

The school day starts at 8.50am and finishes at 3.15pm. Children arriving after 8.50am are recorded as “Late”.

Children may enter school from 8.40am to take part in morning challenge within their classrooms.

Children may attend Breakfast Club from 08.00am

Daily Timetable

The school day at Denbigh Primary School operates from 08:40 to 15:15. After school clubs operate on a daily basis from 15:15 to 16:00. Key Stage 1 children receive fruit during their morning break time and have a second break time in the afternoon.
