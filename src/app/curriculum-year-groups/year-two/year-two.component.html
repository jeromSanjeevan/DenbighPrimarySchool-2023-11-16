Year 2 Overview

Welcome to Year 2 at Denbigh Primary School!

The learning in Year 2 is a continuation of the journey started in EYFS and Year 1. We build upon the children's foundations to ensure that they are secure in key skills that will enable them to flourish as they move into Key Stage 2. We provide an inclusive, engaging and vibrant learning environment through a Continuous Provision approach.

All pupils complete a project each week that enables them to develop their enquiry skills, gives them autonomy over their learning, and allows them to become confident, independent learners. Through small group teaching, all children are encouraged to be resilient and to reach their full potential. In Year 2, children complete their Phonics Screen Check and Standard Assessment Tests (SATs) during the Summer Term.

We ensure that our Year 2 pupils have an engaging and broad curriculum that is based around 6 topics.

Treasure Islands
  • (Geography, Science, History) - Arrrrrrhh! Ahoy there me hearties! Year 2 jump onboard a pirate ship and explore what life during The Golden Age of Piracy and about the places that Pirates sailed to. We learn how to use maps to identify the continents of the world and we learn about our own area through local walks and by drawing maps.
The Magic Toy Maker
  • (History, Science, D.T.) - We step into a toy shop and learn about how toys have changed through the decades including the types of materials they are made from. We also sew to create our very own teddy bear.
We Are What We Eat
  • (Science, Geography, D.T.) - This is when Year 2 step into the kitchen and become master chefs! We will learn about how to live a healthy life style and where our food comes from.
I'm Alive!
  • (Science, Geography) - This is where we learn about how plants and animals live and grow. We will learn about food chains and life cycles. We even get to watch our very own chicks hatch and grow!
People of the Past
  • (History) - We step back into the past and learn about key figures from British History. Pupils will also learn about historic buildings such as castles through a visit to Warwick Castle.
The Earth Our Home
  • (Science, DT, Geography, Computing) - Where we enjoy the outdoors and identify the places that plants and animals live. We will learn about how different places on our planet have different climates and how this influences the plants and animals that live there. Year 2 will visit Marston Vale Forest Centre to become ecologists and explore pond and meadow habitats.
