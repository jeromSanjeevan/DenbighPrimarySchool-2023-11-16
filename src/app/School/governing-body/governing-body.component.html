The Governing Body

The Governing Body has to make sure our school meets the needs of every child so they achieve as well as they possibly can. Together with the headteacher, the Governing Body agrees how the school grows and improves (strategic development), makes sure children learn and achieve to their best ability (accountability) and checks the work of the school (monitoring and evaluation).

As a school we have the following committees:

  • Full Governing Body
  • Finance Personnel & Premises Committee
  • Achievement & Standards
